Who We Are

CRNA-Led Staffing Built on Transparency and Reliability

Anesthesia Coverage Experts, PLLC was established by CRNAs who understand the clinical, operational, and administrative realities of anesthesia practice. Our model ensures staffing solutions that reflect real clinical experience — professionally managed and scalable to meet evolving facility needs.

Headquartered in Raleigh, North Carolina, we partner with healthcare facilities across the United States. Our CRNA-owned and operated structure means every decision is informed by the same standards we hold ourselves to as providers.

As we grow, our commitment remains unchanged: experienced providers, dependable coverage, and professional support at every step.
